LOS SANTOS RACING LEAGUE

ORIGINALLY FOUNDED BY MICHAEL FARRELL AND AIYA ACETI, THE LOS SANTOS RACING LEAGUE WAS CREATED IN THE SUMMER OF 2021 AS A WAY TO BRING THEIR FREIENDS TOGETHER FOR A SUMMER EVENT: AN EVENT ALL ABOUT VIRTUAL RACING WITH TRACK CARS, BASICALLY NASCAR BUT VIRTUAL. WITH ENOUGH PEOPLE ON BOARD, THIS VIRTUAL LEAGUE QUICKLY TOOK OFF AN BECAME THE HIGHLIGHT OF THE 2021 SUMMER. THOUGH AS TIME WENT ON, THE LEAGUE BECAME QUIET. SOON ENOUGH, THE LEAGUE WAS TEMPORARILY SHUT DOWN.

HOWEVER, FARRELL AND ACETI ARE LOOKING TO REVEIE THE LEAGUE. WITH INTEREST COMING BACK SLOWLY AND WITH A SHARP REBRAND, THE REVIVAL OF THE LEAGUE IS LOOKING TO GO SOMEHWERE PROMISING.

LSRL WAS ALWAYS MEANT TO BE A PLACE WHERE LOVERS OF RACING COULD COME ENJOY AND EXPERIENCE LIKE NO OTHER . WE DESIGNED LSRL TO BE MORE THAN JUST RACING, WE DESIGNED IT TO BE A HOME FOR FRIENDS AND ALL OTHERS WHO ENJOY THE SPORT OF RACING.

SO COME JOIN US ON OUR REBRAND AND REVIVAL ADVENTURE AS WE LOOK TO RESTART AND EVENT THAT BROUGHT SO MANY OF US TOGETHER. WITH THAT BEING SAID, LET'S RIDE!!
